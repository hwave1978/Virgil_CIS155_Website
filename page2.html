What Inspires You?

Looking for inspiration in your garden? How about taking a page from your neighbor’s cat, who thinks your flower beds are the latest litter box? Nothing says "garden chic" like a surprise bouquet of wilted daisies sprinkled with a little feline fertilizer! Forget about manicured lawns; the real trend is a chaotic patch of “natural” decor, complete with paw prints and the occasional buried treasure. Just embrace it—your garden might not win any awards, but it’ll definitely be the talk of the town (and the neighborhood cats will have a blast!).
